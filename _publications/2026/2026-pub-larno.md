abstract: >-
  We propose a novel neural operator named LarNO (pronounced “Lar-no”) that enables zero-shot generalization to high resolutions, thereby achieving memory-efficient training 	of models for urban flood dynamics. LarNO is the first method to model millions of spatial mesh cells and hundreds of millions of spatiotemporal points, surpassing existing studies by one order of magnitude. LarNO achieves mm-level depth accuracy and ∼940× acceleration in inference speed compared to the hydrodynamic model.
