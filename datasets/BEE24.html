BEE24 challenges models to track multiple similar-appearing small objects with complex motions over long periods. It serves as a testbed for MOT research in more complex and diverse real-world scenes, significantly contributing to tracking general objects, including pedestrians, vehicles, drones, animals, insects, etc.

The dataset is designed to expose limitations of existing trackers under complex motion patterns, occlusion, and appearance similarity, pushing the boundary of multi-object tracking research in unconstrained environments.
